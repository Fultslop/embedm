
Example of the synopsis plugin
==============================
 
## Frequency / En / 5 sentences

```yaml embedm
type: synopsis
source: ./chapter_1_loomings.txt
max_sentences: 5
algorithm: frequency
language: en
```

## Luhn / En / 5 sentences

```yaml embedm
type: synopsis
source: ./chapter_1_loomings.txt
max_sentences: 5
algorithm: luhn
language: en
```
